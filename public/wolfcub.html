Wolfcub digital agency


Project description

I like challenging myself so I decided to recreate Wolfcub. The website has a simplistic modern layout with smooth animations that gives the website more depth.

Just like with any project, there were certain hurdles to overcome. I focused only on the client-side and not the back-end. I wanted to recreate the site that performs well and has good SEO optimization and best practices.

I have used most resources found on the original site and added personal touches to certain sections of the site.
